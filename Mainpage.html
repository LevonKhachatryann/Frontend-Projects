Essential Characteristics of Old Money Style
Old money style is known for its enduring appeal. It embraces classic silhouettes, clean lines, and timeless pieces that can be worn for years without going out of style. From luxurious fabrics to well-tailored garments, attention to detail is key. It favors subtle luxury over flamboyant displays, emphasizing refined simplicity rather than excessive embellishments.
Old money style values high-quality materials and impeccable craftsmanship. Instead of fast fashion items, those who embrace this style invest in high-quality pieces that are built to last. To accomplish the old money aesthetic, focus on curating a wardrobe and selecting pieces that can be worn for years.
